options: PktZabicieMin: 1 PktZabicieMax: 3

command /efekt [] []: trigger: arg 1 is "fireprot" or "fp": arg 2 is "kup": player has permission "efekt.ochrona.kup" or "efekty.VIP.kup": {efekty.%player%.ilosc} is greater than 10: apply fire resistance to the player for 480 seconds send "&6* &7Otrzymales &6Ochrone przed Ogniem &7na &c8 Minut &7!" remove 10 from {efekty.%player%.ilosc} stop else: send "&6* &7Brakuje ci punktow zeby to kupic !" stop arg 1 is "regeneracja" or "rege": arg 2 is "kup": player has permission "efekt.regeneracja.kup" or "efekty.VIP.kup": {efekty.%player%.ilosc} is greater than 15: apply regeneration to the player for 480 seconds send "&6* &7Otrzymales &dRegeneracje &7na &c8 Minut &7!" remove 15 from {efekty.%player%.ilosc} stop else: send "&6* &7Brakuje ci punktow zeby to kupic !" stop arg 1 is "punkty" or "p" or "pkt": player has permission "efekt.punkty.sprawdz": send "&6* &7Twoje Punkty : &c%{efekty.%player%.zabicia}%" stop arg 1 is "sila": arg 2 is "kup": player has permission "efekt.sila.kup" or "efekty.VIP.kup": {ochrona.kup.%player%.zabicia} is greater than 10: apply strength to the player for 480 seconds send "&6* &7Otrzymales &4Sile &7na &c8 minut&7 !" remove 10 from {ochrona.kup.%player%.zabicia} stop else: send "&6* &7Brakuje ci punktow zeby to kupic !" stop arg 1 is "help": arg 2 is not set: player has permission "efekt.help": send "&8------------------[ &9CommandEfect &8]------------------" send "&3/efekt fireprot kup &7- Kupujesz ochrone przed ogniem na 8 Minut" send "&3/efekt sila kup &7- Kupujesz sile na 8 min" send "&3/efekt protection kup &7- Kupujesz ochrone na 8 Minut" send "&3/efekt tworca &7- Wszyscy tworcy skryptu" stop arg 1 is "autor" or "tworca": send "&8------------------[ &9CommandEfect &8]------------------" send "&3Tworcy &7: eX. i duchdamian [DjDuch]" send "&3Wersja &7: 0.1" stop if arg 1 is not set: if arg 2 is not set: make player execute command "efekt help" stop
